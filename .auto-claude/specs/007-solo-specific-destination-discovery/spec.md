# Solo-Specific Destination Discovery

AI-powered destination and activity discovery specifically for solo travelers with safety ratings, solo-suitability filters, personalized recommendations, and comprehensive search. Includes popular solo destinations, hidden gems, and safety insights.

## Rationale
Addresses user pain point: 'Difficulty finding destinations, accommodations, and activities suitable for solo travelers.' Fills market gap (gap-7) - general travel apps don't filter for solo-suitable options. This is a key differentiator that leverages AI for solo-travel-specific insights.

## User Stories
- As a solo traveler, I want to discover destinations that are safe and enjoyable for solo exploration
- As a user, I want safety ratings and insights so that I can make informed decisions about where to travel
- As a planner, I want personalized recommendations based on my travel style and preferences

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Search functionality filters destinations by solo-suitability ratings
- [ ] Safety scores and insights visible for each destination
- [ ] Personalized recommendations based on user preferences and past trips
- [ ] Curated lists of popular solo destinations and hidden gems
- [ ] Users can save destinations to their trips or wishlist
- [ ] Filters for budget, activity level, safety, and solo-friendliness
- [ ] Integration with trip planning for easy addition to itineraries
